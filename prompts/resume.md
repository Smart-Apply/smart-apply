{{summary}}

{{experiences}}

{{education}}

## Certifications:

## Instructions:
1. Create a professional, ATS-friendly resume (max 2 pages)
2. Structure:
   - Header: Name, contact information
   - Professional Summary: 2-3 sentences highlighting key qualifications
   - Technical Skills: Organized by category (Languages, Frameworks, Tools, etc.)
   - Professional Experience: Most recent first, with bullet points for achievements
   - Education: Degree, institution, year
   - Certifications: Name, issuer, date
   - Projects: Key personal or professional projects

3. Best Practices:
   - Use action verbs (Built, Designed, Implemented, Led, etc.)
   - Quantify achievements with metrics (%, numbers, scale)
   - Prioritize relevant experience for the target role
   - Keep bullet points concise and impactful
   - Use consistent formatting throughout

4. Format: Clean markdown with clear sections
5. Length: Aim for 1-2 pages total
6. Tone: Professional, confident, results-oriented

Generate a resume that highlights the candidate's strongest qualifications and is optimized for both ATS systems and human readers.
